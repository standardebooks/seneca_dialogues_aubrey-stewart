Colophon

Dialogues
is compiled from letters and essays written between 40 and 63 AD by
Lucius Annaeus Seneca.
It was translated from Latin between 1887 and 1898 by
Aubrey Stewart.

This ebook was produced for
Standard Ebooks
by
Alex Cabal,
and is based on transcriptions produced between 2007 and 2011 for
Wikisource,
on transcriptions of footnotes produced in 2016 by
Alex Cabal,
and on digital scans from the
Internet Archive.

The cover page is adapted from
The Roman Ruins in Schönbrunn,
a painting completed in 1832 by
Ferdinand Georg Waldmüller.
The cover and title pages feature the
League Spartan and Sorts Mill Goudy
typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by
The League of Moveable Type.

August 3, 2016, 2:07 a.m.
